About Us

Founded in 2010, Sirish Royal Enfield Store has been the trusted dealer for Royal Enfield motorcycles in the region. Our team is passionate about bikes and committed to providing the best customer experience.

History

  • Founded in 1901 in Redditch, England.
  • Supplied motorcycles in World Wars.
  • Bullet 1948 still in production.
  • 1955: Factory established in India.

Modern Era

  • Classic 350/500
  • Himalayan
  • Interceptor & GT 650

Philosophy

Pure Motorcycling. Focused on freedom, adventure, and connection with the bike.

Culture

Events like Himalayan Odyssey and custom scene support.
